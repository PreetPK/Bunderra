Grazing Properties

Bunderra Cattle Co has selected properties to grow the highest-quality beef.

Bunderra, or ‘Among the Hills’ is an organic beef operation with over half a million hectares of some of the best grazing country in Queensland and the Northern Territory.

Our breeding operation centers around our Northern Territory holdings at Murranji Station. Once weaned, cattle are brought south to live out their days on exceptionally beautiful fattening country in the foothills of the Great Dividing Range. Our pastures are regenerative, nutrient-rich and fed by natural springs.
